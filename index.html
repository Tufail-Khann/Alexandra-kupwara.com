SCHOOL ORGANISATION (CONCEPT)

A.S.S was established in the year 2000, at Kupwara. School is a little reverence to the contributions by different brains for Human civilization. It is not merely an institution but a vision a vision of conglomeration of educationists, technocrats, philosophers, writers etc. To renovate the absolute educational systems around the global village. The School aims at promoting quality education discipline initiative creativity among children. Our main aim is to offer quality education as well as to take care of moral, physical, mental and cultural education of our children. Besides academic and extra -curricular activities the school emphasises on a crucial aspect of personality building and character formation Since one of our primary aims is to develop qualities of body, mind and character which will enable children of today to become good and worthy citizens of tomorrow.


The school is affiliated to the board of school education. The school also boasts of a unique system of education quite different from other schools. We call it a preventive system of education contrary to the repressive system of education. This includes reason, God and loving kindness. Education through loving manner not having a philosophy to punish a child. Admissions are made on the basis of written test and interview of the child and interactions with parents. Rear powers and policies emanating from management percolates down to various compartments of the institute in a very systematic fashion. It is necessary to mention that each department of institute is accountable to chairman Alexandra School directly or indirectly.
